HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Controls

Player 1: WASD + Z rotate + S drop

Player 2: ← → ↑ ↓ + / rotate + ↓ drop

🧩 Matching

Clear full horizontal lines to score and send garbage to your opponent.

Match 4+ same-token clusters for chain explosions and bonus damage!

📈 Live Prices

Pumping tokens fall faster and give bonus points.

Volatile tokens become wild blocks — trigger bigger explosions!

⚔ Attacks

1 line clear → no attack

2 lines → send 1 garbage row

3 lines → send 2 garbage rows

4 lines (CLASH!) → send 4 garbage rows 💥

Cluster bomb → send 3 garbage rows 💣
